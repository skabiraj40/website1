Tour Itinerary

Day 01 : Bangkok - Pattaya

Arrival at Bangkok Airport. Proceed towards Pattaya. Arrive and check-in at a hotel. Day at leisure. Night stay at hotel.

Day 02 : Pattaya

Breakfast. Set out for Coral Island Tour. Enjoy a nice Indian Lunch in the afternoon. Get back to hotel for overnight stay.

Day 03 : Pattaya - Bangkok

Proceed towards Bangkok after breakfast. Arrival & check-into a hotel. Day at leisure. All night long stay at hotel.

Day 04 : Bangkok

Breakfast. Get ready for a Half Day sightseeing tour of the Temples & City. Get back to hotel for all night long stay.

Day 05 : Departure

Breakfast. Check-out from Airport.
