Travel Bali With Us

Tour Itinerary

Day 01 : Arrive in Bali

Upon arrival at Ngurah Rai International Airport (Denpasar International Airport), you will be transferred to the hotel. Check-in into the hotel and relax. Enjoy rest of the time in leisure. Do dinner in the evening and stay overnight.

Day 02 : Romantic dinner cruise

Breakfast. Have a healthy breakfast in the morning and then get ready to have a refreshing spa treatment. After this session you will be transferred to the hotel. In the afternoon you will be transferred to the Benoa harbor. Board on cruise to enjoy sunset while cruising. Watch traditional Duck performances, cabaret show and dinner. After cruise, return to the hotel for overnight stay. Enjoy Dinner.

Day 03 : Kecak-Uluwatu Experience

Breakfast.After a healthy breakfast in the hotel, enjoy rest of the time in leisure. In the evening have a great Balinese sunset experience. You will be driven to Uluwatu. Visit the holy temple of Pura Uluwatu and then enjoy a Balinese Kecak dance performance. Enjoy a scrumptious dinner in night and return to the hotel for overnight stay.

Day 04 : Depart from Bali

Breakfast. Have a healthy breakfast and then check-out from the hotel. You will be transferred to the airport for departure.

Day 05 : Departure

Breakfast in the hotel. Check out and transfer to Bali Airport for your return flight.
