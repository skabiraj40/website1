Travel Goa With Us

Tour Itinerary

Day 01 : Arrive in Goa

Welcome to Goa! Check in to your hotel and spend the day just as you like.

Day 02 : Sightseeing in North Goa

Breakfast. The second day of your Goa tour package will take you to exploration of the gems in North Goa

Get to visit some of the best beaches of Goa like Baga, Candolim, Calangute and Dona Paula. Collect seashells, write your name on the sand, relax in the shacks and do just what you like as you explore.

Day 03 : Sightseeing in South Goa

Relish a refreshing breakfast and embark on South Goa tour. Start off with a visit to Shri Shantadurga Temple in Kavlem and Shri Manguesh Temple in Priol. Continue towards the churches and convents of Goa (UNESCO World Heritage Site) like Basilica of Bom Jesus, Church of Our Lady and Se Cathedral.

Have your lunch and relax in Dona Paula and Miramar Beach. Make your holiday in Goa more memorable by shopping until you drop at Panaji market.

Day 04 : Dudhsagar Falls, jeep safari and spice plantation tour

Begin the fourth day with mouth-watering breakfast and take the much-awaited trip to Dudhsagar. Enjoy jeep safari as you pass through lush forests to visit the falls. Splash around the milky waterfall and treat your eyes to the beauty.

Day 05 : Leisure day

Breakfast. The fifth day of this Goa tour itinerary is all yours. Spend it just as you like.

Day 06 : Departure

Have your breakfast and check out from the hotel. Get assisted transfer to airport and catch your flight home.
